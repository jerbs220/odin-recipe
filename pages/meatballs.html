Meatballs

Simple Meatball Delight Ingredients: 1 pound (450g) ground beef 1/2 cup breadcrumbs 1/4 cup milk 1 egg 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1 cup marinara sauce Instructions: Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C). Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, beaten egg, salt, and black pepper. Mix well with your hands. Shape Meatballs: Form small meatballs (about 1 inch in diameter) and place them on a baking sheet. Bake: Put the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes until the meatballs are cooked through and golden brown. Warm Marinara Sauce: While the meatballs are baking, heat the marinara sauce in a pot on the stove. Combine and Simmer: Once the meatballs are done, gently place them in the pot with the warm marinara sauce. Let them simmer together for an additional 10 minutes. Serve: Your easy and delicious meatballs are ready! Serve them on their own, with pasta, or as a sub sandwich. Enjoy the simplicity and savory goodness of these homemade meatballs!
