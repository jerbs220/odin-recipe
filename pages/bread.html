Homemade Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water and sugar. Sprinkle yeast over the water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Stir the mixture until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.
  5. Punch down the dough, shape it into a loaf, and place it in a greased loaf pan. Cover and let it rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the bread for 25-30 minutes or until it sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  7. Allow the bread to cool before slicing. Enjoy your homemade bread!
